Refund Policy

Last updated: October 1, 2025

1. Refund Eligibility

Refunds are only available under the following circumstances:

  • If WAEC is unable to process your results upgrade request
  • If there is an error in the payment processing
  • If the service is not delivered within the promised timeframe

2. Non-Refundable Circumstances

Refunds will not be issued in the following cases:

  • If the examination board rejects the upgrade request
  • If incorrect information was provided by the candidate
  • If the candidate changes their mind after payment
  • If the upgrade request is already in progress

3. Refund Process

To request a refund:

  1. Contact our support team at support@waec.com
  2. Provide your transaction details and reason for refund
  3. Allow 7-14 business days for processing

4. Processing Time

Approved refunds will be processed within 14 business days. The time it takes for the refund to reflect in your account may vary depending on your payment method and financial institution.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our refund policy, please contact us at support@waec.com.
